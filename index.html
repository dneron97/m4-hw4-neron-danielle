Spotlight on Taylor Swift

My favorite singer is Taylor Swift! Even though I love listening to a variety of artists, I chose Taylor for a few different reasons. First, I have been a fan of hers for a long time. Second, I like the majority of her music. While I have some preferences, I generally enjoy her music style. Third, I like that the lyrics are relatable, and Taylor does a great job of connecting with her fans. Keep reading to learn more about Taylor!

About Taylor Swift

Taylor is a country and pop artist who has been in the industry since 2006. Taylor writes her music, which helps her stand out more. Throughout her career, she has won multiple awards, including Grammy's. Outside of music, Taylor has established residency in several major cities: Nashville, Los Angeles, and New York City. Taylor also has three cats to keep her company.

Discography

Taylor has recorded nine 9 original albums. A few of them are Fearless, Lover, 1989, and Folklore. Fearless, Lover, and 1989 are all pop. Folklore was released in 2020, which ended up being very successful. It is Alternative/indie, which is something new. I ended up loving the album, and I listened to it profusely during quarantine. My favorite song is Exile!

Fun Facts
